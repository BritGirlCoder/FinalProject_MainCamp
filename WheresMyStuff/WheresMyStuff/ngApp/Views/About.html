Why "Where's my Stuff"?

The founders of Where's my Stuff move about as often as most people take vacation. On average, they've moved once a year for the past 15 years.

Every move meant packing up everything they owned into boxes. And no matter how well the boxes were labeled, there were times when they just couldn't find that widget. Sometimes not every box got unpacked right away and they forgot they already owned the widget and bought another.

Our goal at Where's My Stuff? is to help you keep track of what you have and where it is. Upload the name and description of your item, its photo, where it is, and never lose an item again. You'll know that your rarely used craft supplies are in box 3 in the storage unit, and that your baby photos are in box 10 in the garage.
